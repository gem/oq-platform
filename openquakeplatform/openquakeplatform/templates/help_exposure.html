About

The database structure is built to contain information on buildings and people from the country-level all the way down to the individual building. The first version of the Global Exposure Database (GED) will contain aggregate information on population and the number/built area/reconstruction cost of residential and non-residential buildings at a 1km resolution. Detailed datasets on single buildings will be available for a selected number of areas and will increase over time. Read more ...

The Building Exposure Export tool is an interactive map created by GEM that allows users to download a subset of the Global Exposure Database. Users can also interacte with building fractions, viewing building distribution on the fly. The application also includes GRUMP urban and rural, IMPRO, NERA, GA and Hazus layers
